Independent Bookstores in NY

The reason why I wanted to create this website is because I wanted to showcase independent bookstores around New York. So the three bookstores around NYU that I visited was the Greenlight Bookstore and McNalley Jackson Bookstore which are in Brooklyn, near Tandon and then I also visited Mercer Street Books which is in Manhattan, near West Fourth. Each of these bookstores offered something unique and had a different atmosphere than the others.
